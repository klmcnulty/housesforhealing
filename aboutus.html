About Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to care for the sick by providing low cost, temporary housing to those who are receiving long-term medical treatment away from home.

We are expressing the healing love and grace of Jesus Christ through individuals, businesses, and churches across denominational lines bringing unity through common vision to the Body of Christ

How can you help?

Houses For Healing is now building its first house. We are currently raising funds to secure land and build an additional nineteen houses for the sick. We pray for a total of twenty houses to serve the twenty surrounding communities around Abilene, TX. Each house will be for the primary benefit of a targeted community and each of the targeted communities is a neighbor to Abilene.

Houses For Healing is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, a ministry of Sonrise Ministries

We own and maintain all the facilities/tiny houses. We also facilitate engagement between the adopting churches and Good Samaritans for the sake of the ones in need.

Function of Houses For Healing:

  • Manage and maintain properties
  • Responsible for taxes, utilities, cable, upkeep, lawn, plumbing, appliances, mattresses, minor repairs, reservations, processing applications, secure cleaning fees, and provide access
  • Pastoral care as needed
  • Raising funds for upkeep, building tiny houses and the chapel
